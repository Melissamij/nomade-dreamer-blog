About

Bonjour !

To make it short before my travel timeline, I am an Island, Sun, Ocean lover. I dive and freedive, dream of becoming a surfer one day.
I am a foodie, always looking for the best places to have a food experience.

Planet lover, so I try to make the most concious choices

I am 32 years old et I am touch all. I was born in 1990 in Cannes and spend my summer time up in the mountain.
At the age of 19 I left to visit Brazil and Israel. My two first major travel, that changed me and made me a travel addict.

In 2012, after saving money for two years working in restaurants, I left for Australia by my self. I spend a full year there and became a barista while studying Interior Design.
It was my richest travel experience, living abroad for the first time, learning a new language, meeting new cultures.

For the next two years, I worked during the summer in Cannes and travelled during winter. Lived a winter in London, travelled around South Est Asia...

2016, I went to New Zealand with a Working Holliday Visa, lived in a van and worked as Barista for 6months.

Then in 2018, I wanted to learn more about coffee so I left for Colombia & Panama. Well something special happened, I met my current partner in crime, a Danish Men.So 6 months later I moved to Denmark.

Since 2018, I have been living in Denmark.
